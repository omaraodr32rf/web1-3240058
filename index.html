Wasfi Mustafa Wahbi AL-tal

Wasfi al-Tal was born in 1920 in Irbid, Jordan. He was the son of the famous poet and politician Mustafa Wahbi al-Tal (Arar). He received his education in Jordan and Palestine before studying at the American University of Beirut.

Prime Minister of the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan

Throughout his political career, Wasfi al-Tal was known for his strong sense of nationalism, discipline, and dedication to public service. He held several ministerial positions and served multiple terms as Prime Minister. His leadership was marked by efforts to strengthen state institutions and promote national unity.

Early Life and Education

Wasfi al-Tal spent his early years in Irbid before moving to Palestine for his studies. He later joined the American University of Beirut.
