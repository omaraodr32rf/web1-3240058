University of Jordan - Aqaba Campus

About the Aqaba Campus

The University of Jordan Aqaba Campus is part of the University of Jordan, located in the southern city of Aqaba. It focuses on providing quality education tailored to the needs of the region and emphasizes applied sciences and technology.

  • Campus Established: 2000s
  • Location: Aqaba, Jordan
  • Faculties: Engineering, Business, and Applied Sciences
  • Focus: Regional development and applied research

Campus Life

The Aqaba Campus offers a vibrant student life with academic clubs, sports programs, and community initiatives. The campus features modern classrooms, laboratories, and recreational areas to support students' academic and social development.
