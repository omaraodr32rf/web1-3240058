Football World

Football, also known as soccer, is the most popular sport in the world. It brings people together, creates unforgettable moments, and inspires millions of fans globally. The sport is played professionally and recreationally, and it has a rich history that spans over a century.

  • Origin: 19th century, England
  • Number of players: 11 per team
  • Main event: FIFA World Cup
  • Governing body: FIFA
  • Famous leagues: English Premier League, La Liga, Bundesliga, Serie A

Rules of Football

The main objective of football is to score more goals than the opposing team within 90 minutes. The game consists of two halves of 45 minutes each, and if a match is tied, extra time or penalty shootouts may decide the winner. Players are not allowed to touch the ball with their hands except for the goalkeeper within the penalty area.
