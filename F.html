About Islam

Islam is a religion of peace and goodness, based on worshiping Allah alone and following the teachings of Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him). It includes belief in Allah, angels, holy books, prophets, the Day of Judgment, and divine destiny.

Values & Principles

  • Honesty and integrity
  • Justice and equality
  • Forgiveness and compassion
  • Good behavior towards others
  • Pursuit of knowledge and continuous learning
